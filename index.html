1. Introduction

Welcome to CivilPulse ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the CivilPulse mobile application ("App").

By using CivilPulse, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use CivilPulse:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from Google.
  • Usage Data: Articles you read, categories you browse, and how you interact with the app.
  • Quiz Data: Your daily quiz attempts, scores, and answer history.
  • Bookmarks: Articles you save for later reading.
  • Streak & Progress: Your daily usage streak and quiz performance statistics.

We do not collect: your device's precise location, contacts, camera/microphone access, payment information, or any sensitive personal information.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To save your bookmarks and quiz history across devices
  • To display your progress, streak, and quiz scores
  • To improve the app's content and features
  • To send notifications about daily quiz availability (if you opt in)

4. Third-Party Services

CivilPulse uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — Database and authentication hosting. Your data is stored on Supabase's secure servers. See Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Google Sign-In — Used for authentication. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Services — For app distribution and updates.

We do not sell your data to advertisers or any third parties.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase's servers with the following protections:

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Row-level security ensures you can only access your own data
  • Passwords are never stored — we use Google OAuth only

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will delete your profile, bookmarks, and quiz history within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — Update your profile information
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Opt-out — Disable push notifications at any time in your device settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

CivilPulse is designed for users aged 18 and above (competitive exam aspirants). We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have accidentally collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
